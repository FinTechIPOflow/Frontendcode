Frequently Asked Questions?

Find answers to common questions that you or others interested in IPO.

What is an IPO?

+

IPO, or Initial Public Offering, is the first time a company's shares are offered to the public. As an investor, you will now be able to subscribe for shares in the company. The company uses the capital raised from the IPO for various purposes like expansion, debt repayment, etc.

How to invest in an IPO?

+

You can invest in an IPO through your BLUESTOCK account. Simply navigate to the IPO section, select the IPO you're interested in, and click "Apply". You'll need to provide your UPI ID to complete the application process.

What is the benefit of an IPO?

+

IPOs offer several benefits including the opportunity to invest in promising companies at an early stage, potential for listing gains, and long-term growth prospects. Additionally, IPO shares are often offered at a discount to their expected market value.

What are the disadvantages of an IPO?

+

IPOs can be risky as there is limited historical data available for the company as a public entity. The stock price can be volatile after listing, and there's no guarantee of allotment due to oversubscription in popular IPOs.

What is the difference between book building issue and fixed price issue?

+

In a book building issue, the price is discovered through bidding within a price band, while in a fixed price issue, the company sets a specific price for the shares. Most modern IPOs use the book building method as it allows for better price discovery.

Is it mandatory to have a PAN number to apply in an IPO?

+

Yes, it is mandatory to have a PAN (Permanent Account Number) to apply for an IPO in India. The PAN is required for KYC (Know Your Customer) verification and tax purposes.

Where do I get an IPO application form?

+

With BLUESTOCK, you don't need a physical application form. You can apply for IPOs directly through our platform by selecting the IPO and clicking the "Apply" button.

How one can apply in IPO's online?

+

To apply for an IPO online through BLUESTOCK, log in to your account, navigate to the IPO section, select the IPO you want to invest in, enter the number of lots you wish to apply for, and complete the payment using UPI.

How one can apply in IPO's offline?

+

To apply offline, you need to obtain a physical ASBA form from your bank, fill it out with your details and IPO information, and submit it to your bank branch. However, online application is much more convenient and recommended.

Can a minor apply in an IPO?

+

No, minors (individuals below 18 years of age) cannot directly apply for an IPO. However, a guardian can apply on behalf of a minor through a demat account opened in the minor's name with the guardian as the operator.
